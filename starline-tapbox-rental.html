Tap-off boxes are used to draw power from the busway and have a special plug head (paddle) which inserts into the busway track slot. Twist the tap-off box 90 degrees to make electrical connection. The blades in the paddle assembly fit into the busbar channel inside the busway track for a compression connection.

These provide a connection system for overhead power distribution networks, so that a load bank and cable can be used to commissioning the busway and verify operation.

Compatible with the B400T5 series busway, and rated for 600V 400A.

Weight: 40lbs. Dimensions: 8x12x20 inches (LxWxH)

For maximum safety follow NFPA 70e guidelines for proper installation and de-installation of tap-off boxes from busway power distribution systems.
